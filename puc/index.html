Introduction.(play)

Every secondhand piece, I post online, gets its own tag at the secondhand store. It makes it easier for people to find the piece in the specific shop.

Inspiration for the project - "Sustainability in Fashion", my BA graphic design thesis where I questioned the green vawe of fashion advertising and consumption.

Who is the girl in the mirror?(play)

--- Sustainability might seem like an elite lifestyle, especially in fashion. Thrifting is the best option and not the most popular one in Czechia. With my project, I am breaking the negative stigma by showing cool, secondhand pieces that are available at Prague, via an Instagram profile.

Graphic designer, art director and content creator.

PUCPUC is a one (wo)man creative studio based in Prague, lead by Patrícia Potančoková (born in Slovakia). I am an independent creative °freelancer based in Prague. I focus on visual identities, specific design products and online experiences for brands and artists who want to stand out and step up. I do this by asking the right questions, applying intuition and engaging my design peers with progressive design thinking. So even though I work by myself, I never technically work alone because I have build a creative super–support system around me.
